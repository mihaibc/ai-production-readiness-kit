# Head of AI 90-Day Plan

Use this plan to move from scattered AI experiments to a repeatable production-readiness operating model. It is written for leaders who need portfolio visibility without slowing every team with heavyweight process.

## Days 1-30: Baseline

- Inventory active AI use cases.
- Classify each use case by risk, data sensitivity, and business value.
- Identify workflows already in production or pilot.
- Create common intake, eval, observability, and governance expectations.
- Find the highest-risk gaps in RAG, human review, and access control.

## Days 31-60: Operating Model

- Define launch gates for pilot and production.
- Assign product, engineering, data, and operational owners.
- Create a reusable scorecard and risk register.
- Standardize prompt, model, and retrieval change review.
- Build reporting for cost, quality, latency, and adoption.

## Days 61-90: Scale

- Move suitable workflows from pilot to controlled production.
- Stop or redesign workflows with weak value or unacceptable risk.
- Create reusable patterns for RAG, evals, approvals, and observability.
- Train teams on the production readiness process.
- Publish a regular AI portfolio review.

## Success Signal

By day 90, teams should be able to show which AI workflows are in demo, pilot, controlled production, or blocked status, and why.
