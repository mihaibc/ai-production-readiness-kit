## Required Controls

- Use-case intake before delivery starts
- Data classification before implementation
- Privacy and legal review for sensitive workflows
- RBAC for restricted data
- Human approval for high-risk outputs
- Eval evidence before pilot and production
- Observability for cost, latency, quality, and errors
- Change review for prompts, models, tools, and retrieval sources

## Decision Rights

- Who can approve pilot launch?
- Who can approve production launch?
- Who can approve model or provider changes?
- Who can pause or roll back the workflow?
