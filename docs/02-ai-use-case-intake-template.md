# AI Use Case Intake Template

## Use Case

- Name:
- Owner:
- Stage:
- Primary users:
- Workflow description:

## Business Value

- What problem does this solve?
- What happens today without AI?
- What measurable outcome should improve?
- Is there revenue, cost, risk, speed, or quality impact?
- Will outputs go outside the company or to customers?

## Data

- What sources are required?
- Who owns each source?
- What is the data classification?
- Is any PII, confidential, regulated, or customer data involved?
- How fresh does the data need to be?

## Controls

- Who can use the workflow?
- Who can access the underlying documents?
- Who approves high-risk output?
- What audit trail is required?
- What legal, privacy, or compliance review is needed?

## Delivery

- What counts as a successful pilot?
- What tests must pass before launch?
- Who owns incidents and rollback?
- How will cost, latency, and failures be monitored?
