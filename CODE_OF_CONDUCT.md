# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This project is intended to be a practical, respectful space for people working on production AI readiness, governance, evals, observability, and delivery quality.

Everyone participating in this project is expected to communicate with professionalism, curiosity, and respect.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different backgrounds, experience levels, and viewpoints.
- Give feedback that is specific, constructive, and focused on the work.
- Assume good intent, but be willing to clarify and correct misunderstandings.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project and its goals.
- Respect privacy, confidentiality, and legal boundaries when sharing examples.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private, confidential, or identifying information without permission.
- Dismissing or belittling people based on background, seniority, employer, geography, or role.
- Repeated off-topic, disruptive, or bad-faith participation.
- Submitting examples that expose confidential customer, employer, or personal data.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that does not fit this code of conduct, open a private channel with the project maintainer where possible. If a public issue or discussion contains sensitive material, avoid amplifying it and ask for it to be removed.

Reports should include:

- what happened
- where it happened
- who was involved
- any relevant links or screenshots

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove content, close discussions, reject contributions, or block participants when behavior harms the project or its community.

The goal is not to police disagreement. The goal is to keep the project useful, safe, and professional.
