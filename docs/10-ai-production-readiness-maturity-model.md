# AI Production Readiness Maturity Model

## Level 1: Demo

- Works on selected examples.
- Prompts and data paths are informal.
- No reliable evals, observability, or ownership.

## Level 2: Pilot

- Clear use case and initial users.
- Basic data and security review.
- Some human review.
- Early evals and cost tracking.

## Level 3: Controlled Production

- Defined launch gates.
- Versioned prompts and controlled retrieval.
- Human approval for high-risk outputs.
- Monitoring for cost, latency, errors, and quality.
- Rollback and incident ownership defined.

## Level 4: Scaled Production

- Standard architecture patterns.
- Regression testing for model, prompt, and retrieval changes.
- Portfolio-level governance.
- Adoption and business impact measured.

## Level 5: Optimized

- Continuous quality improvement from feedback and evals.
- Cost, risk, and value are managed as an operating system.
- Teams can safely evolve models, data, prompts, and workflows.
